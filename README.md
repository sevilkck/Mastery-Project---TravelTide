# Mastery-Project---TravelTide
# Traveler Segmentation & Revenue Insights

## Project Overview
This project analyzes traveler segmentation, behavioral patterns, and revenue opportunities across five key user groups: Family Travelers, Business Travelers, Single/Couple Frequent Travelers, Weekender Travelers, and Other. The analysis informs targeted strategies to maximize conversion, loyalty, and growth for a travel booking platform.

## Objectives
- Identify and profile major traveler segments.
- Quantify segment distribution and revenue potential.
- Uncover behavioral patterns and discount sensitivity.
- Recommend targeted strategies for each segment to drive conversion and revenue.

## Tools & Skills Used
- **Databricks Notebooks**
- **Python** (data analysis, visualization)
- **Markdown** (documentation, reporting)
- **Data Visualization** (bar charts, radar charts)
- **Segmentation Analysis**
- **Business Intelligence**
- **Statistical Analysis**

## Methodology
1. **Data Segmentation:** Travelers grouped by behavioral and booking patterns.
2. **KPI Analysis:** Key metrics (page clicks, flight fare, hotel nights, bookings) compared across segments.
3. **Visualization:** Bar and radar charts used to illustrate discount sensitivity and behavioral fingerprints.
4. **Insight Generation:** Segment-specific recommendations and business insights documented.

## Key Findings
- **Family Travelers** (42.8%) are the largest segment, driving long-stay, high-value bookings—ideal for bundled offers and family-friendly perks.
- **Single/Couple Frequent Travelers** (31.0%) are highly engaged, booking often and responding well to personalized recommendations and loyalty rewards.
- **Business Travelers** (11.7%) generate the highest revenue per user, with frequent bookings and premium spend—best served by convenience and premium perks.
- **Weekender Travelers** (4.1%) are an under-served, low-frequency group with high growth potential via aggressive short-stay deals.
- **Other** (10.3%) are heavy browsers but low converters, requiring pricing incentives and retargeting.
- **Discount Sensitivity:** Family and Weekender Travelers are most responsive to hotel and flight discounts; Business Travelers rely on corporate rates.

## Assessment
The analysis provides actionable insights for targeted marketing and product development. Family and Frequent Travelers are core revenue drivers, while Business Travelers deliver high-value bookings. Weekender and Other segments present untapped growth opportunities through tailored incentives. Adopting segment-specific strategies will maximize conversion, loyalty, and overall revenue for the platform.
